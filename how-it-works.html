‚ùì How It Works FAQ

How does the AI actually create music? +

DeepRabbit.net uses advanced neural networks trained on thousands of hours of professional music. The AI understands musical patterns, harmony, and rhythm, then generates new music based on your style selections and weight settings.

Think of it like having a virtual musician who knows every genre and can instantly create music in any style you choose.

Why do I need to adjust weight sliders? +

Weight sliders control how much each style influences the final music. Higher weights make that style more prominent, while lower weights make it subtle or background.

This lets you create unique blends - like "80% Jazz, 20% Electronic" for a jazz-electronic fusion sound.

What happens when I click EVOLVE? +

EVOLVE mode automatically changes your music over time. It gradually shifts melodies, rhythms, and harmonies while maintaining the overall style and mood you've chosen.

This creates dynamic, evolving music that never gets repetitive - perfect for background music, live performance, or inspiration.

Can I control the music with MIDI? +

Yes! DeepRabbit.net supports MIDI controllers. You can map MIDI knobs and sliders to control style weights, tempo, and other parameters in real-time.

This gives you hands-on control for live performance, just like playing a traditional instrument.

How does the mobile interface work? +

The mobile interface uses touch-optimized controls. Swipe up from the bottom to access menus, tap and drag to adjust sliders, and use the bottom navigation for quick access to main functions.

All features work the same on mobile as desktop - just with touch-friendly controls.

What makes DeepRabbit.net different from other AI music tools? +

DeepRabbit.net focuses on real-time performance and live control. Most AI music tools create static compositions, but DeepRabbit.net lets you perform and evolve music live, just like playing an instrument.

Plus, it's completely free, works in any browser, and doesn't require any downloads or installations.
