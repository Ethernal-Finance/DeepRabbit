üéµ Creating Your First Track

Follow this step-by-step guide to create your first professional track with DeepRabbit.net.

1. Choose Your Foundation

Start with 2-3 styles that work well together. Here are some great combinations:

Style Combination Primary Style Secondary Style Accent Style
Electronic House Ambient Synthwave
Organic Jazz Blues Folk
Experimental Techno Lo-Fi Trap

2. Set Initial Weights

1

Primary Style

Set weight to 1.5-2.0 for the main genre that will drive your track.

2

Secondary Style

Set weight to 0.5-1.0 for supporting elements and texture.

3

Accent Style

Set weight to 0.2-0.5 for subtle flavor and unique character.

3. Hit Play and Listen

Click the play button and let the AI generate the foundation. Listen for 30-60 seconds to hear the direction and feel of your track.

Listening Tips

Pay attention to the rhythm, melody, and overall mood. This will help you decide what adjustments to make next.

4. Adjust in Real-Time

Increase Weights

Move sliders up for more influence from that style.

Decrease Weights

Move sliders down for subtlety and background presence.

Toggle Styles

Turn styles on/off for dramatic changes and transitions.

Add More Styles

Introduce additional genres gradually for complexity.

5. Record When Ready

Click record when you like what you hear. Perform live adjustments while recording to capture the evolution of your track.

Congratulations!

You've created your first track with DeepRabbit.net! Experiment with different style combinations and weight settings to develop your unique sound.
